Current Water Restrictions

For Homosassa Special Water District Customers

Effective March 10, 2012

Addresses with "house numbers" May irrigate on:
Ending in 0 or 1 Monday
Ending in 2 or 3 Tuesday
Ending in 4 or 5 Wednesday
Ending in 6 or 7 Thursday
Ending in 8 or 9 Friday

Watering is allowed before 8:00 A.M. or after 6:00 P.M. No watering is allowed on Saturday or Sunday.

This is for watering from, public water supply, well, river or canal.
